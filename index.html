Frictionless multi-modal notes

Take any note anytime during any step of the experiment without disrupting your workflow. Easily turn your raw note for presentation and sharing.

Conduct your most complex experiments with live step-wise guidance. Summon a variety of easily accessible built-in tools while multitasking.
